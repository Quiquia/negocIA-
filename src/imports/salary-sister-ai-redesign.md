Refine and redesign the Salary Sister AI prototype to make the experience emotionally impactful, visually engaging, and clearly connected to the problem of gender pay inequality.

IMPORTANT LANGUAGE RULE:

All interface text must remain in Spanish.

CORE MESSAGE TO COMMUNICATE

Women around the world often earn less than men for similar work.

Many women also feel insecure negotiating salaries due to:

• lack of salary transparency
• lack of negotiation training
• social and cultural biases
• fear of asking for higher compensation

Salary Sister AI exists to help women understand their value and negotiate with confidence.

The design should clearly communicate this mission.
