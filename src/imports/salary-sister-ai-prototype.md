PRODUCT NAME

Salary Sister AI

TAGLINE

“Conoce tu valor. Negocia con confianza.”

PRODUCT MISSION

Salary Sister AI helps women discover their real market salary value, identify salary gaps, and practice salary negotiation using AI.

The goal is to reduce the gender pay gap by giving women **data, confidence, and negotiation tools**.

The product must communicate **financial empowerment and transparency**.

---

TARGET USERS

Women professionals who want to:

• understand if they are underpaid
• gain confidence negotiating salary
• learn negotiation strategies
• close the gender pay gap
